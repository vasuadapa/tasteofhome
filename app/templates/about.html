ABOUT TASTE OF HOME

Taste Of Home is your local Indian tiffin delivery service in Melbourne serving the best tiffin to students and workers who have little to no time for preparing their food. We deliver a personalised eating experience from the comfort of your home with our daily and monthly tiffin delivery subscription.

Cooked with love and care, every dish on our tiffin menu is cooked perfectly with quality ingredients and local produce. We never compromise with health and hygiene.
