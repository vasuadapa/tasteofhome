WELCOME TO TASTE OF HOME

Delivering Homemade Indian Meals In Tiffin

Craving for home style food on your lunch and dinner plate? Taste Of Home serves the best Indian tiffin in Melbourne and surroundings. Every dish is prepared by expert chefs having hands-on experience in cooking home style Indian meals delivered to your doorstep every Monday to Friday. Whether you are craving for North Indian vegetarian meal or South Indian non-vegetarian thali, we pack them hot and fresh in tiffin and deliver to you. Pickups are also available from our facility.

Hot & Fresh Indian Tiffin Service

Everything we serve is cooked fresh and with love in a professional kitchen. We use the local produce and quality ingredients to make sure the food is delicious and relishing. Our menu is different every day and we never repeat dishes for a week so that you can experience the taste of recipes all around from India. We never compromise with health and hygiene and promise to deliver good food.
